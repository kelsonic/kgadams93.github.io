About Me

Italy

Born and raised in Colorado, I sought to study outside of the United States immediately after high school to learn more about the world. I decided to go to Rome, Italy, where I studied at John Cabot University (an American university).

Netherlands

I graduated with a Bachelor's of Arts in International Affairs and a Bachelor's of Arts in International Business. Desiring to get experience in the tech startup world, I moved to the Amsterdam-area in the Netherlands. I joined a tech startup called Bomberbot, whose mission it is to teach kids how to code.

Bomberbot: Teaching kids to code.

I was inspired by the team's CEO, who recommended me to get into programming. Upon that advice, I joined 3+ Ruby on Rails online bootcamps, which gave me a great overview of how to make Rails apps. That said, I still felt as though I was missing formal education in programming.

That's when I came across Dev Bootcamp.

Upon acceptance into DBC, I did a little dance and, since then, I have been preparing for the move from the Netherlands to San Francisco, California. My mission is to learn even more about Rails and to become a professional Ruby on Rails developer.
